About Us

National Blood Transfusion Service (NBTS) Sri Lanka is a special campaign under the ministry of health. It has a unique role providing timely supply of quality assured blood and blood components and related laboratory, clinical, academic and research services for the entire government sector hospitals and for the majority of the private sector hospital in the country.

Our Team

Staff

Roles

staff members play a crucial role in facilitating the donation process from start to finish. This includes organizing donation drives, registering donors, conducting pre-donation screenings to ensure eligibility, collecting blood samples, and providing post-donation care

Hospitals

Support

Hospitals are key stakeholders in the blood donation system as they rely on donated blood to meet the needs of patients requiring transfusions. They work closely with blood banks and donation centers to maintain an adequate supply of blood and blood products for various medical procedures, including surgeries, trauma care, cancer treatments, and managing chronic conditions.

Organizations

Contribution

Various organizations contribute to the blood donation system by advocating for donation awareness, organizing donation campaigns, and supporting blood banks and hospitals in their efforts to collect and distribute blood. These organizations include national and international blood donor associations, non-profit organizations, community groups, corporate entities, and government agencies.
